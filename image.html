Diamond specs

Physical Properties:

    Chemical Composition: Pure carbon (C)

    Crystal Structure: Isometric (cubic) crystal system

    Hardness: 10 on the Mohs hardness scale (the highest rating)

    Density: 3.52 g/cm³

    Refractive Index: 2.42 (high refractive index, which gives diamonds their brilliance)

    Dispersion: 0.044 (high dispersion, which gives diamonds their fire)

    Luster: Adamantine (highly reflective and shiny)

    Cleavage: Octahedral cleavage (diamonds can be split along certain planes)

Optical Properties:

    Refractive Index: 2.42 (as mentioned earlier)

    Dispersion: 0.044 (as mentioned earlier)

    Brilliance: High brilliance due to high refractive index and dispersion

    Fire: High fire due to high dispersion

    Luster: Adamantine (highly reflective and shiny)
